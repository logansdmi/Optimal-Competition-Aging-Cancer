Cell populations equations

Fitness is a measure of how well a cell can reproduce and it depends on the vigor V (availability of resources), base rate f0 and the fraction k of cooperation c (how much of the resources the cell gives others).

For healthy cells (h): V=c=1

For cancerous cells (c): V=1,c=0

For senescent cells (s): V=0,c=1

For cells that are both senescent and cancerous (b): V=c=0

Using new term βi to set removal rates of the different types of cells.
